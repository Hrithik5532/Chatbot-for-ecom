Introduction
------------

Welcome to Eshtyle by Zenrevo IT Solutions, a cutting-edge fashion e-commerce platform designed to offer personalized shopping experiences through customization and AI-driven design generation. Our mission is to revolutionize the way you engage with fashion, providing a space for creators to showcase their designs and for customers to create their unique style.

Information Collection
----------------------

We collect various types of personal information, including:

*   Name and contact details
*   Payment information
*   Customization preferences and choices
*   Images and designs uploaded by creators and users

Data is collected directly from users, through cookies, and other technologies to enhance user experience and improve our services.

Use of Information
------------------

The collected information is used to:

*   Process transactions and customize products
*   Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
*   Generate AI-driven designs based on user preferences

All designs and customizations are processed with the utmost care to ensure your visions are accurately brought to life.

Sharing of Information
----------------------

Information may be shared with third parties under the following circumstances:

*   Vendors for product customization
*   Business partners for plugin integration

User designs may also be featured on the platform, with proper consent, to inspire the community.

Data Protection
---------------

We employ various measures to protect user data, including encryption and access control. Our data retention and deletion policies ensure your information is handled responsibly.

User Rights
-----------

Users have the right to access, correct, or delete their personal information. For any such requests, please contact us through the information provided below.

Third-Party Services
--------------------

Our platform may include services from third parties, whose privacy practices are not covered by our policy. We encourage users to review these policies.

International Data Transfers
----------------------------

Information may be transferred internationally. We ensure all data is protected with appropriate safeguards.

Changes to the Privacy Policy
-----------------------------

Any changes to our privacy policy will be communicated through our website and via email, where applicable.

Contact Information
-------------------

For any queries or concerns regarding the prelaunch campaign for Eshtyle or these Terms and Conditions, please contact us at support@eshtyle.com.
